2.4.1 Auto-attack

The auto-attack feature is nice, especially for those who will be using melee combat extensively. The default key for auto-attack is the [ Q ] key. When you press it, your character will begin to attack your current target, if it is in melee range.

** NOTE: You may chose to change some of the default keymap settings to be something a little more to your preference. To do this, first press ALT+O, and then select the tab at the top of the new window that is marked "Keyboard".
