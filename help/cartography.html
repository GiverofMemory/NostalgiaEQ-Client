5.2 Cartography

To open the Map window, use backspace or click on the Compass icon on the Window Selector.

You can scroll the map using the arrow buttons at the top right of the window.

To zoom in or out on the map you can use the mouse wheel, the buttons labeled with + and - sign, and the pyramids below the Arrow buttons.

There are four map layers. The Base layer contains any maps created by SOE. The other three layers (1, 2 and 3) are for use by players to create their own maps or additions to the ones provided.

You can toggle any of these layers by clicking on the appropriate buttons under "Layers: Visible" heading.

When drawing maps, you can choose which of the three available layers you wish to draw on by selecting that layer under the Active heading.

Labels can be toggled on and off using the Labels button.

The Height Filter button toggles the height filter. When toggled on, the height filter will only display lines on the map that are within the Low and High levels. The high and low values are calculated above and below your character.

The Compass icon at the bottom right of the Map window opens the mapping toolbar. Use this toolbar to create your own maps.
