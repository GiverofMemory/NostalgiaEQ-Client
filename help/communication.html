2.2 Communication

The chat window will be your main means of communication. There are several different chat channels, noted below is a list of each and its intended use. To chat, press enter, type your message, and press enter again to send the message.

* /shout (message) - Zonewide channel - This should be used sparingly, when you wish to get several other peoples attention.

* /auction (message) - Zonewide channel - This is used when you have goods to sell that are useful to other players.

* /ooc (message) - Zonewide channel - This is used for most of the out of character chatter that goes on in game.

* /say (message) - Local Channel - This is used for talking to those in your immediate area.

* /gsay (message) - Groupwide Channel - This is used for talking to your group mates.

* /guild (message) - Guildwide Channel - This is used to speak to everyone in your guild, regardless of what zone they are in.

** NOTE: This assumes that your chat is set to use /say as the default mode. That is the default setting.

** NOTE: However you choose to communicate, please refrain from using inappropriate language.
