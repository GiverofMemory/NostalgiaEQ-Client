2.4.2 The "/Con" System

The /Con system is a means for a player to consider the level of difficulty of his opponent. To consider something, right click on your target. You may also left-click on your target, then type /con, they both work identically. When you consider something, you will receive one of six colored messages back. Below is a listing of those colors and what they mean.

* Green - This target is significantly below your skill level.

* Light Blue - This target is significantly below your skill level.

* Blue - This target is an adequate challenge.

* White - This target is of equivalent skill, and is a great challenge.

* Yellow - This target is more skilled than you are.

* Red - This target is far more skilled than you are.
