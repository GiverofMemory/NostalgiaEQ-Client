4.1.9 Feedback Commands

/bug or /b - Takes you to a bug-reporting screen.

/feedback - Takes you to the feedback/comments screen.

** NOTE: Please use /bug whenever you see something that is broken in the game, everything from a misspelling in some text to a place where NPCs get stuck.

** NOTE: /feedback should be used for suggestions or comments, not bugs. If you want to suggest a change to a spell, NPC, zone or anything like that, please use /feedback.
