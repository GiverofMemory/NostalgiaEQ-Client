3.6 Grouping

The underlying appeal in a game like EverQuest is the social interaction between players, and grouping is a great way to start getting to know new people. If you are looking for a group and wish to inform others, type /lfg on. This will toggle a flag next to your name both in a /who and on the nametag above your head.

When you find someone that you would like to group with, you may invite them to your group by targeting them and pressing CTRL+I. When you wish to leave a group, press CTRL+D to disband the group.

** NOTE: If you are the group leader, CTRL+D will disband the person whom you have targeted from the group.
