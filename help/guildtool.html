5.3 Guild Management

To open the Guild Management window, use ALT-G or click on the Angle Brackets icon in the Window Selector.

You can sort the guild listing by clicking on the header of the column that you want to sort from.

The names of guild members that are online will be displayed in white, those that are not online will be displayed in gray.

Public Notes can only be created by guild leaders and officers. Public Notes are visible to every guild member.

Private Notes can be created by anyone in the guild. Private Notes are only visible to the player that created them, no other member of the guild can see them.

To create a note (Public or Private), click on the appropriate guild members name on the list. Then type the note into the appropriate box and click the Update button next to it.

The guild's Message of the Day (MOTD) can be created or updated by typing the message into the MOTD box and clicking Update.

The Promote, Demote and Make Leader buttons are only available to guild leaders.

To remove a member from the guild, the guild leader just clicks on the name of the member to be removed and clicks the Remove button.
