1.0 Welcome to EverQuest - Welcome to EverQuest, a world of mystery, adventure, and perils unknown! Before you get to work exterminating the local fire beetle infestation, vanquishing the local Orc menace, or even sharing your tales of bravery with your friends, please familiarize yourself with this manual. If you are new to EverQuest, or have just purchased one of the expansions, this manual contains information that you may find vital on your adventures.
