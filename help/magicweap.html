3.3 Magical Weapons

There are a number of monsters that you will encounter that require a magical weapon to harm them. If you do not have a magical weapon, that target will be invulnerable to you. It is wise to purchase or quest for a magical weapon as soon as you are able.


** NOTE: This does not apply to spells, as spells are innately magical.
