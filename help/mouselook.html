2.1.2 Mouse-look

Mouse-look is a feature that enables you to use the mouse to look around. To utilize mouse-look, you will use the [Right-click-and-drag] method. Click and hold the right mouse button while moving the mouse around.

If you press and hold down the left mouse button while using mouse-look, your character will begin to run in the direction you are facing.
