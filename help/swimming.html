2.1.3 Swimming

Your character will begin swimming when he/she enters the water. You will notice a new gauge on your screen when you are submerged that represents how much air you have left. When you begin to run low on air, use Mouse-look (or the [PageUp] key) to look up, then swim forward to get your head above the water.
