Bank Tips

- To see the name of an item, either in your inventory or in the bank, just point your mouse cursor at the item.
- To see the names of all items in your bank, place your mouse cursor in the bank's inventory window and hold down the ALT key.
- All banks in EverQuest are linked. So you can safely place your goods and cash in the bank in Qeynos and retrieve it all in Freeport when you arrive.
- The banker will change your coins for you. If you take 20 copper pieces and drop them into the bank window where it indicates silver pieces are stored, the banker will automatically change your 20 copper into 2 silver.
- Click on the "Change" button to convert all your coin, both in the bank and on your person, to the fewest possible coins.
- Clicking on the banker with an item on your cursor is giving that item to the banker, not storing it in your bank. So make sure to open the bank window and place the items in the bank.
