Barter Search Tips

This window is used to search for Barter Buyers that want specific items. Your current inventory is listed for convenience.

- To search for Buyers, either click on an item in the Inventory list, or type in a partial name of the item in the text entry area next to the Search button, then click the Search button. The list of Buyers that want items that match your search will display.

- To sort the results of a search, click on the column headers of the Buy Lines list.

- To see what the item compensation of a Buy Line is, click on the Buy Line, and the compensation items (if any) will show in the lower list. To view the stats of a compensation item, click on it, then click the "View Item" button.

- To find a Buyer in the zone, there are two methods. First, you can right-click the Buy Line, and you will orient to face the Buyer. Second, you can click on the Buy Line and click the "Find Buyer" button, which will generate a glowing path to the Buyer.

- To sell to a Buyer, right click on them. This will bring up the Barter Merchant window, with which you can sell to the Buyer.

For more information, please see Help.
