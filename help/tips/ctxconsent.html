Consent Tips

Someone has just given you consent to drag their corpse.

- You can now move their corpse using the /corpse command. Just target the corpse and type /corpse and if you are close enough, the corpse will be dragged to you.
- Use the Social. There is a Corpse social in your action window. Using this can make dragging corpses easier.
- To give someone consent on you own corpse, type /consent {character name}.
- Be careful who you consent. It's easy for someone to place your corpse in a hard to recover location.
- Be polite when given consent. You are playing in a very social environment and if you do something mean to someone, like dragging their corpse to a bad spot, you can harm your reputation.

For more information, please see Help.
