World Container Tips

You have opened a "world container" such as a forge or an oven.

- Do not leave items in a world container. A world container can be accessed by anyone, so if you leave something in a loom it may not be there when you come back.
- When working on recipes, make sure not to stack items. Some recipes will not work with items in a stack. Hold down CTRL when picking up items out of your inventory to pick up one at a time.

For more information, please see Help.
