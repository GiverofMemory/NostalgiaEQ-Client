Friends List Tips

- You can easily see if your friends are online. The Who button will activate a /who all on everyone on your friends list. Or you can click on one of the names on the list and the Who button will check just that name.
- Adding people to your Ignore list prevents you from seeing their text. So if they are trying to make peace with you, you may not know it. Be careful about who you choose to ignore.

For more information, please see Help.
