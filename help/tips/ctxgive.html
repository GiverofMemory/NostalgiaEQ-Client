Give Tips

You have started to give something to an NPC by clicking on that NPC with an item on your cursor.

- If you don't intend to give anything to the NPC, click Cancel.
- If you are giving the NPC quest items, make certain that the items are not stacked. Hold down CTRL when picking up items out of your inventory to pick up one at a time.
- Pets can use some items. Your pet might be able to benefit from a weapon or some armor.

For more information, please see Help.
