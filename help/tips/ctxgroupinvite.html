Group Tips

You have been invited to join a group.

- To accept the invitation, click the Follow button in the Group window (or press Ctrl-I).
- To decline the invitation, click the Decline button in the group window (or press Crtl-D).
- If you want to start your own group, just target someone (when you are not in a group yourself) and click Invite in the group window (or press Ctrl-I).

For more information, please see the Help.
