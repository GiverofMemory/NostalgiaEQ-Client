Guild Bank Tips

This window displays what is currently in your Guild Bank; allowing normal members to view the contents, deposit new items and withdraw items marked for them, as well as allowing Guild Bankers to manipulate bank items and their withdrawal permissions.

Guild Member Tips

- To view an item, right-click the item, or click on the item and then click View Item button.
- To deposit an item, pick up the item from your inventory and click the Deposit button.
- To withdraw an item, click on the item in the Bank area and click the Withdraw button. Note that you will only be able to withdraw items that are listed in white.

Guild Banker Tips

- To accept deposits, click on the item in the Deposits area and click Put In Bank.

- To set withdrawal permissions, click an item in the Bank area, then select the permission type. Only Members that match the permission type will be able to withdraw that item.
- To change a target member for a Single permission type, click an item in the Bank area, then click Change Member. Enter the name of the member whom you wish to be able to withdraw the item.
- To split stacks, click an item in the Bank area, then click Split Stacks. You can then choose how many of the current stack should be moved into a new one. Ctrl auto-selects 1, Shift auto-selects half the stack.
- To merge stacks, click an item in the Bank area, then click Merge Stacks. All items that can stack with the currently selected item and have the same withdrawal permissions will form as few stacks in the bank as possible.

For more information, please see Help.
