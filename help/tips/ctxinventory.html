Inventory Tips

- To automatically equip an item, place the item into the blank square in the center of the inventory screen. The item will go into the proper equipment slot, if that slot is available, or into your inventory.
- To view detailed information about an item, right-click and hold (or ALT-click) on the item and an information window will appear.
- To read books and notes, simply right-click the book or note item icon.
- To give or trade items with someone, click the item to place it on your cursor, then click on the player or NPC with whom you wish to trade.
- To scribe a spell, open your spellbook, click the spell item to place it on your cursor, and click the empty spellbook slot into which you wish to scribe the spell. Spells may only be scribed once each.

For more information about items and inventory, please see the Help section about Acquiring, Selling and Buying Items.
