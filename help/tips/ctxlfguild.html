Looking for Guild/Looking for Member Tips

This window can be used by a player looking for a guild to describe himself and announce that he's interested in guild membership, as well as by a guild looking for members to specify the classes, level range, etc. that they're looking for in prospective members. Finally, players and guilds can use the search tabs to find either guilds looking for members or players looking for guild that match a specified filter.

LF Guild tab - to be used by players looking for a guild
- To turn on your Looking for Guild flag, specify your play timezone and write a short comment about what kind of player you are or what kind of guild you're interested in, then click the "LFGuild On" button.
- To update your information, simply make changes to your timezone and/or comment, and click "Update Info". You will need to update at least once a week to keep your LFGuild flag on.
- To turn off your LFGuild flag, click the "LFGuild Off" button.

LF Players tab - to be used by guild officers of guilds looking for members
- To turn on Looking for Player's for your guild, specify the level/AA requirements, classes needed, timezone and a comment for prospective members and click "LFPlayers On". Your comment should also contain contact information for players who want to join your guild.
- To update your information, simply make changes to your requirements and/or comment, and click "Update Info". You will need to update at least once a month to keep your guild's LFPlayers flag on.
- To turn off your guild's LFPlayer flag, click the "LFPlayer Off" button.

Search Guilds tab - to be used to search through the list of guilds that have LFPlayers on.

Search Players tab - to be used to search through the list of players that have LFGuild on.

For more information, please see Help.
