Looting Tips

- To loot items most efficiently, right-click on the item on the corpse and it will be placed in your inventory (or equipped if appropriate).
- Coins on the corpse are taken automatically by the first person to loot the corpse.
- Autosplitting (/autosplit) coins is often the fairest way to distribute coins, but talk to your group about this, some people may not want any coins.
- Be polite about looting. Make sure that loot is shared with your group, don't take all the stuff. Also, don't loot corpses that may have been killed by other players without asking first.

For more information, please see Help.
