Mail System Tips

- The mail system icon in the selector window will blink when you receive new messages. It will appear lit (with a yellow background) as long as you have unread messages in your inbox. If the mail system servers are down, it will appear with a red background.
- The current mailbox is by default the one belonging to the character your are playing. You can quickly switch to your other character mailboxes on that server.
- The message list is, by default, sorted by date, the most recent message at the top. You can sort by sender, subject or date by clicking on the corresponding column header. Clicking again on a column header reverses the sorting order.
- You can select multiple messages at once. A mouse click only selects one message at a time. To select a range of messages, click on the first one and SHIFT-click on the last one. You can also individually toggle your selections using CTRL-click.
- You can only reply to, reply to all, or forward one message at a time. The corresponding buttons are only enabled when exactly one message is selected in your inbox.
- You can report inappropriate messages to Customer Service. The report button will make the selected messages accessible to Customer Service.
- The address book and the mail options buttons are found in the upper right corner of the mail window.

For more information, please see Help.
