Potion Belt Tips

To add a potion to the potion belt:
- Click on an active potion belt slot with a potion belt enabled item.

To use a potion from the potion belt:
- Right Click on the slot.
- Use the assigned hotkey for the potion slot
- Use the /potionbelt activate command.

To remove a potion from the potion belt:
- Use the /potionbelt remove command.

Items that can be used in the potion belt will have Potion Belt Enabled in there item display window.
