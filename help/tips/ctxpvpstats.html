PvP Stats Tips

-The top area of the window displays general information concerning your character's PvP history.
         -Infamy is a representation of your PvP skill compared to other players. A higher number indicates more frequent kills, and generally higher skill.
         -Vitality is a indication of how long it has been since you last died. The farther below zero you are, the more recently you have died.
-The lower windows display information concerning your most recent PvP interactions.
-Press the refresh button to update your information.
-Press the Leaderboard button to bring up PvP information regarding other players on the server
