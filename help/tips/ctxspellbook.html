Spellbook Tips

- To scribe a spell into your spellbook, click on a spell scroll in your inventory and then click on an open spell slot in the spellbook.
- To memorize a spell for casting, click on a spell in the spell book and then click on an open spell gem in the spell bar.
- To delete a spell from your spellbook, right-click on the spell and hit the "Delete" key.
- To move a spell in the spellbook, right-click on the spell and then right-click on the destination (you can click on another spell and the two will switch locations)
- To cast a spell, select your target (if needed) and click on the corresponding spell gem on the spell bar.

For more information about spells, please see the Help section about Using Magic.
