Storyline Tips

The Storyline window displays information about the ongoing story of EverQuest, as well as historical tales of Norrath.

- Any time there is a new story the window will open when you log in.
- To prevent the Story window from opening when there is a new tale, uncheck the box on the bottom left of the window.
- The stories are displayed in the order that they are added, so the newest ones are always on the top of the list.

For more information, please see the Help.
