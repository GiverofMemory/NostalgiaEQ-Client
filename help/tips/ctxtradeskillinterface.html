Tradeskill Window Tips

This window can be used to perform any tradeskill recipe you know with much fewer clicks than the old experimental interface.

- To get a list of recipes, enter your search criteria (min and max trivial values and/or a partial name) and click Search. All recipes that you know that can be combined in this container and match your criteria will display in the list.

- To open the container as a bag, click on the Experiment button (or use Ctrl-rightclick to open the bag in the first place). This will give you access to the contents of the container, if you had placed anything in it before.

- To combine a recipe, select a recipe after performing a search, determine whether you have all the components (they should all have a green background, red means you don't have it in your inventory), then click Combine. The results will appear on your cursor, which must be cleared before you can combine again.

- Favorites: You can deem any recipe to be a "favorite" by selecting the recipe and clicking on Add underneath the Favorites label. Favorite recipes that match your container will be displayed immediately when you open the container, and you can re-show your favorites at any time using the Show button. Select a favorite recipe and click Remove to delete it from your favorites list.

- Auto-inventory The Auto-Inventory button works just like the auto-inventory area in your inventory window.

- Use CtrlRClick When this button is un-pressed (the default), then right-clicking on a tradeskill container brings up this interface, and Ctrl-Rightclicking the tradeskill container brings up the old "bag" interface. Depressing the "Use CtrlRclick" button reverses these two.

For more information, please see Help.
