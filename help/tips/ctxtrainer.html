Trainer Tips

- Some skills require that you train the first point before you can improve them through use. So if use isn't showing improvement on a skill, you may want to head back to your trainer to learn the basics.
- Some skills can't be learned until you attain the required level. The Rank column shows the needed to attain those skills.
- You get 5 practice points every level, so you may want to visit your trainer after you hear the "ding" sound for more training.

For more information, please see Help.
