Tribute Benefit Tips

This window displays pertinent Tribute data for your Personal Tribute Benefits, as well as any Benefits your guild may have active.

Personal Tribute tips
- To Activate/Deactivate selected Benefits, click on the Activate/Deactivate button. Note that you will be charged for your Benefits when you Activate them, and each 10 minutes thereafter until you Deactivate them (while logged in).

Guild Tribute tips
- To Activate/Deactivate selected Benefits, click on the Activate/Deactivate button. Only Officers and the Guild Leader have this ability. Note that your Guild will be charged for the Benefits when Activated, and each 10 minutes thereafter until Deactivated (while any guild member is logged in).

- Opting out. You can opt out of Guild Tribute Benefits (you'll receive no benefit, and the guild won't have to pay for you) at any time by pressing the Opt Out button. You will have to visit a Guild Tribute Master to Opt In again.

For more information, please see Help.
