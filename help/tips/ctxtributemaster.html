Tribute Master Tips

This window displays your current Tribute status (favor accumulated, active benefit costs, etc.), as well as giving you the opportunity to activate new benefits and donate items and platinum for more favor. Note that Benefits are active only after being selected and then Activated using the Tribute Benefit window, which may not be done while using the Tribute Master Window.

- To donate an item, click on the item in your inventory and click the Donate button.
- To donate cash (platinum only), click on the platinum button above the Donate button, use the quantity selector to choose an amount, then click the Donate button.
- To select/upgrade benefits, click the benefit in the Master list, then click the Upgrade button until the tier you want is listed under Active Benefits. Note that Benefits have level requirements, which may not allow you to select that tier.
- To unselect/downgrade benefits, click the benefit in either list, then click the Downgrade button until the lower tier you want is listed under Active Benefits, or the Benefit is completely removed from that list.

- Career Favor is the total amount of Favor the character has ever gained.
- Current Favor shows how much Favor is available to use.
- Active Cost is the number of Favor points that will be expended at the next interval.
- Level displayed is the minimum level needed to gain the benefit.
- Cost listed is the number of favor points needed to maintain the benefit.
For more information, please see Help.
