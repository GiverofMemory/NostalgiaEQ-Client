4.1.2 Tracking Commands

/trackfilter (Ranger only) - This command returns a list of arguments. They are the standard /con colors. Specify in the argument which /con colors you do not want to see in your tracking display.

/trackplayers ON/OFF - Allows those with tracking skill to indicate whether or not they want player characters to show up in their tracking list.

/tracksort (Ranger only) - Initially, this will return a list of arguments to be used with this command. Below is a list of the arguments and their functionality.

* Normal - Tracks NPC's how long they have been in the zone (oldest NPCs first).

* Distance - Sorts the NPC's by how close they are, with the closest on the top of the list.

* Consider - Sorts the NPC's by level, with the highest-level NPCs on top.

* Rdistance - Sorts the NPC's by how close they are, with the closest on the bottom of the list.

* Rconsider - Sorts the NPC's by level, with the highest-level NPCs on the bottom of the list.
