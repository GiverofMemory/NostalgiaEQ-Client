2.5.2 Trading and Dropping Items

At some point, you may wish to trade items with another player or NPC. To do so, open your inventory and select the item that you wish to trade. Find the person or NPC you wish to trade with and, click on that person or NPC with the item on your cursor. This will open the trade window. Press the "Trade" button to complete the trade.

** NOTE: When trading items with players, always make sure to get something in return for the item you are trading.

To drop an item, select the item from your inventory and click somewhere on the ground near you. This will drop the item. You will notice a small brown bag at your feet, this is the item that you have dropped. To pick the item back up, click on that small brown bag.

** NOTE: NPCs and other player characters may pick up items that you drop.
