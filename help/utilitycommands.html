4.1.4 Utility Commands

/anon - This toggles your anonymous status. When anonymous, your level, race, class, and guild information are not displayed on a /who or /who all.
/roleplay - This functions exactly the same as /anon, except your guild tag will be visible.

/afk - This sets your characters status as "Away From Keyboard", alerting anyone who sends you a tell via an automated response. You may customize the response by typing /afk MESSAGE.

/corpse - This drags the targeted corpse, if you have consent to do so.

/consent Playername - This allows the specified player to drag your corpse to safety.

/dismount - This causes you to dismount your mount.

/dynamiclights ON/OFF - When toggled, this command enables the game to specially render lighting. This is particularly useful when the character has some form of a light source equipped or in their inventory.

/em Message - This performs an emote with the custom message. /em is very hungry, will display "Charactername is very hungry."
/me Message - This operates the exact same as /em.

/facepick - This is a Luclin only option, and will allow you to customize the facial features of your character.

/follow - This causes your character to follow the targeted player, if they are in the same group.

/lfg ON/OFF - This toggles your looking for group status. This is displayed on your name, both above your head and in a /who all.

/loc - This gives you the coordinates of your current location.

/played - This returns your characters total played time.

/note Text - This adds the specified text to a file entitles Notes.txt in your EverQuest directory. These may only be referenced outside of EverQuest, or while EverQuest is in windowed mode.

/random x, y - This rolls a randomly generated number between the specified numbers.

/sit - This toggles your character between the sit and stand positions.

/split # # # # - This splits a user defined amount of money with your group members in the same zone, Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Copper, respectively.

/surname Surname - Once a character reaches 20th level, you are able to assign that character a last name. Once you have chosen a last name, you will not be able to change it, so choose your name with care.

/targetgroupbuff - This command allows you to cast group buffs on the group of the person you are targeting, so long as they are in range of that target. This only works on group buffs with a duration, and will not affect heals, portals, or illusion/change form spells.

/time - This command tells you what time it is, both the current in game time and your local time (based on the clock on your computer.)
